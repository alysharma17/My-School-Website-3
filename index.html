Campus Life

Campus life is great, some of the best places on the campus would be 1. The library (the most peaceful and quiet place in the school where there is more than 1,000 books of so many genres, it is also the location of the computer lab.) 2. The field area (which is a lot of grass and trees located next to the playground) 3. the classroom area (when it's about the school election season, you see lots of banners that the 6th-8th graders have put up to become things like the school president, school coordinator, etc.) A fun thing that happened at the school was in the winter of 2015, when we were invited to the library on the day before winter break, the library was decorated like the Polar Express, we were called over to sit down and the librarian read the Polar Express.

Achievments

Butte Vista School is one of twelve elementary schools in the Yuba City Unified School District, which also includes two intermediate schools, two comprehensive high schools, one continuation high school, and one alternative school. Butte Vista School serves students with a variety of educational and physical needs that are integrated into age appropriate classrooms and activities. Classrooms consist of kindergarten through eighth grade general education, plus specially equipped classrooms for severely handicapped children.
